Controls:
- WASD and mouse to move
- E to interact with objects
- Spacebar to jump
- CTRL to crouch
- Shift to run
- Right mouse button to zoom camera in
- T to open textbook
- R and L to flip textbook pages
- E to enter and F to finish using a Tip PC
